If you run into issues or have questions about SnapRAID, these are the best places to ask for help:

  • Official SnapRAID Forum (SourceForge) - bug reports, development discussion, user questions.
  • r/Snapraid on Reddit - community Q&A, experience sharing, tips.
  • OpenMediaVault Forum - for users combining SnapRAID with OMV setups.
  • UNRAID Forum (SnapRAID plugin) - discussion and support for plugin usage.

Before posting, check existing threads, many questions are already answered. When you post, include your SnapRAID version, your OS, configuration file, error logs or output, and steps you already tried.
