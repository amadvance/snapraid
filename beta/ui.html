The SnapRAID Daemon is the modern companion service designed to move your storage from a manual, terminal-based workflow to an "always-on" background intelligence.

Why the Daemon?

The "Set-and-Forget" Philosophy

The daemon handles the heavy lifting so you don't have to. It proactively monitors array health and manages the parity lifecycle in the background. If it detects a massive accidental deletion or a hardware error, it triggers a Safety Freeze, protecting your parity until you intervene. It is the intelligence that knows when to work and the restraint to stay silent when your disks are idle.

The Modern Visual Experience

Move your management from the terminal to the browser. The integrated Web UI provides a high-fidelity command center that is served natively by the daemon, no complex web server setup required.

Feature Spotlight

Autonomous Maintenance

SnapRAID on Autopilot

Stop managing cron jobs. The daemon handles the full lifecycle of up, diff, sync, and scrub based on your custom schedule, ensuring your parity is never stale.

Active Health Guard

Listen to Your Disks

Beyond parity, the daemon watches the hardware. With integrated SMART monitoring, it detects pre-failure symptoms and rising temperatures before a drive goes dark.

Intelligent Power Management

Spin-down Aware Intelligence

Intelligent logic ensures disks not accessed by applications are automatically spun down. Routine health probes respect standby states to save energy and hardware life.

Safety Interlocks

Defend Against the Accidental

Massive accidental deletion? The daemon's safety thresholds will freeze a sync task instantly, protecting your parity from reflecting a disaster

Multi-Channel Alerts

In the Loop, Everywhere

Whether it's a successful scrub or a critical disk error, get notified via ntfy.sh webhooks, email, or system logs the moment it happens.

Open for Integration

The Terminal, Reimagined for Developers

Full REST API backend designed for third-party integration. Connect your array to Home Assistant, custom scripts, or mobile apps.
